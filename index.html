Welcome to

Central Academy for State Forest Service

The Central Academy for State Forest Service primarily focuses on providing training to Indian Forest Service (IFS) officers and other forest officers from different states. The academy plays a crucial role in enhancing the knowledge and skills of forest officers in various areas such as forest management, conservation, wildlife protection, environmental governance, and sustainable development. The training programs conducted by CASFOS aim to equip forest officers with the necessary tools to effectively manage and conserve forest resources.
