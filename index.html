Brief Introduction

The Central Academy for State Forest Service, Coimbatore is one of the premier institutions under the aegis of Directorate of Forest Education, Ministry of Environment, Forests and Climate Change, which professional Induction training to newly recruited State Forest Officers (ACF) and Forest Range Officers (FRO) from various states and offers in State Forest Service Officers of DCF, ACF, FRO and Deputy FRO ranks.

With the advent of various developmental schemes in the Forestry sector during the IV and V Five year plans, and launching of the Social Forestry Projects in many States, the Government of India felt the urgency of starting two more institutions to train the increasing number of officers specially from the State Services, and as a sequel to this the State Forest Service College, Coimbatore was established on 25th January, 1980. Later it was rechristened as Central Academy for State Forest Service (CASFOS).


Mission & Objective

  • To bring out Forestry Professionals capable of meeting sphere of Forests, Wildlife & Environment sharing.
  • Strengthening existing management process and disseminating new concepts through continued education, in the shape of in-service course to augument his/her managerial skills with administrative & technical acumen
  • Conducting special and refresher courses covering emerging issues in forestry research and technology.
  • Re-orienting basis of forest education in tune with requisite parameters of ecology and environment.

Training

The ACF training program is scheduled for two years and it is eighteen months for FRO’s. After successful completion of the course the Trainees are awarded Honours’ Diploma and Pass Diploma. The training courses cover a wide range of Forestry Subjects, Study Tours, Field Exercises and Qualifying Tests.
